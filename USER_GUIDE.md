# Personal Task Manager – User Guide

Welcome to the Personal Task Manager. This guide walks you through all the features.

---

## 1. Sign in

1. Go to the application URL.
2. Click the **Sign in with Google** button.
3. Select your Google account.
4. You will be taken to your dashboard.

> **Tip:** Your session stays active even if you refresh the page. Click your avatar → **Sign out** when you are done.

---

## 2. Your Dashboard

The main page shows:

- **Statistics cards** – total tasks, completed, pending, overdue, due today.
- **Quick filter buttons** – *Today*, *Overdue*, *This week*.
- **Search bar** – type any word to search task titles and descriptions.
- **Task table** (desktop) / **task cards** (mobile) – see all your tasks.

---

## 3. Managing Tasks

### ➕ Create a Task

1. Click **Add Task** (sidebar on desktop, hamburger menu on mobile).
2. Fill in the **Title** (required), **Description**, **Due Date**, **Priority**, and **Category**.
3. Click **Create Task**.
4. A green toast message confirms the task was created.

### ✏️ Edit a Task

1. In the task list, click the **pencil icon** on the task row (or card).
2. Modify any field.
3. Click **Save Changes**.
4. A green toast confirms the update.

### 🗑️ Delete a Task

1. Click the **trash icon** on the task row (or card).
2. Confirm the deletion in the popup.
3. A green toast confirms the task was deleted.

### ✅ Mark as Complete / Pending

- Click the **circle / checkmark icon** next to the status.
- The task status toggles between *pending* and *completed*.
- A toast message confirms the change.

---

## 4. Searching and Filtering

- **Search box** – filters tasks by title or description as you type.
- **Quick filters** – click *Today*, *Overdue*, or *This week* to see only those tasks.
- **Column sorting** (desktop only) – click any column header (Title, Due Date, Priority, Status) to sort ascending/descending.

---

## 5. Notifications (Reminders)

When a task is due within the next 24 hours, a notification appears.

- The **bell icon** at the top right shows a red badge with the number of unread notifications.
- Click the bell to open the notification panel.
- Each notification shows the task title and due date.
- Click the **X** on a notification to dismiss it.
- Dismissed notifications are removed from the list, and a green toast confirms it.

---

## 6. Sign Out

Click your **avatar** (top right) → **Sign out**. You will be returned to the login screen.
